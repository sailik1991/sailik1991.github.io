Home

Once upon a time, Sailik was born in the city of Kolkata. Over the years, he has developed a taste for life-long learning, thinking, coding, reading, clicking pictures, eating lots of food and listening to a wide variety of music.

After completing his Ph.D. at Arizona State University with Dr. Subbarao Kambhampati, he joined Amazon AI's Lex team as an Applied Research Scientist. He is interested in identifying failure points of cyber systems (eg. deep learning models, cloud systems, web-applications) and proposing solutions that can make these systems behave is a robust and reliable manner. In 2018, he was awarded the prestigious IBM Ph.D. Fellowship for his work.

Feel free to reach out to him with queries relating to his work (or just say a 'hi'). You can download a copy of his CV here.

He likes to talk (a lot) and spends a lot of time doing so, oftentimes, on random topics completely unrelated to his research interests. He is self-critical about his shortcomings and feels correcting them will help him become a better person.

The pronunciation of his name in his native language, Bengali, is Sho-e-lik. It means the aesthetic quality of any work.
